Overview

Our goal is for all graduates to be excellent clinicians, ready to enter any practice, whether they choose fellowship training or independent practice. Our Leadership Education and Development (LEAD) program includes a Quality and Patient Safety certification program, and Master's level courses in Health Care Administration, Health Care Informatics, and Public Health. Research tracks provide opportunities to develop skills in research, scientific presentation, and publication skills.
